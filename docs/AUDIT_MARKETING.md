# SHI Audit: Marketing Appeal Analysis

**Date:** 2026-05-20

---

## Marketing Assessment

### Score: 6.5/10 - Good (with significant upside)

SHI has **strong technical differentiation** but **weak go-to-market execution**. The product is impressive; the packaging needs work.

---

### Current Positioning

> "Solana Holder Intelligence - Probabilistic, explainable intelligence on token holder risks"

## Marketing Strengths

### 1. Technical Credibility
- Cambridge DS course background
- Academic methodology (Cox PH, HMM, Bayesian)
- Published PRD with frozen specifications
- Versioned, reproducible metrics

### 2. Differentiation
- "Dynamical intelligence" vs static snapshots
- Genuinely difficult to replicate
- First-mover in Solana holder analytics

### 3. Community Fit
- Telegram-native (where Solana traders live)
- SWEENEE dashboard proves execution
- Mobile-optimized summaries

---

## Marketing Weaknesses

### 1. No Public Presence
- No landing page
- No documentation site
- No social media accounts
- No blog/content marketing

### 2. Complex Value Prop
- "Cox Proportional Hazards" doesn't resonate
- "Bayesian uncertainty" is too academic
- Need simpler messaging

### 3. No Case Studies
- No public success stories
- No "we detected X rug" testimonials
- No accuracy metrics published

### 4. Limited Accessibility
- Telegram-only limits discovery
- No free tier defined
- No self-service onboarding

## Competitive Moat

### Hard to Replicate

1. **Domain expertise** - Survival analysis + graph theory + crypto
2. **Data moat** - Historical holder snapshots
3. **Model training** - Labeled rug dataset (to build)
4. **Network effects** - Watchlist sharing, community alerts

### Easy to Replicate

1. Basic holder metrics (HHI, Gini)
2. Top holder lists
3. Transaction feeds

**Recommendation:** Double down on predictive models and real-time intelligence—these are defensible.

---

## Conclusion

SHI has **excellent product-market fit potential** but needs **marketing execution**. The technical differentiation is real; it just needs to be communicated in accessible terms.

**Immediate priority:** Landing page + Twitter presence + one compelling case study.
